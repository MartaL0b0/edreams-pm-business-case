# Product Manager Business Case

## General Context

You are a Senior Product Manager for the Company [EDreams] (<https://www.edreamsodigeo.com/>). You have been assigned to a new team and you need to demonstrate your seniority and capacity to deliver your best job and get a promotion.

As a Senior Product Manager for EDreams, you work under the companies cultural values and expectations, which can be found in:

- <https://www.edreamsodigeocareers.com/working-together/>
- <https://www.edreamsodigeocareers.com/edo-life/>
- <https://www.edreamsodigeocareers.com/about-us/>

Everything you deliver, you must do it taking this into consideration.

You are a strong technical PM with a focus on Customer Obsession. You are familiarized with the company's market context and the main competitors and look after launching features and products that follow your team's mission.
You work using the Design Thinking framework and you structure your work with it.

## Project Context

You are the Senior PM for a pod (team) called "Plus services".
The team is made up of: 1 PM (you), 1 UX designer and researcher, 6 developers (frontend and backend) and 1 QA developer.

Your pod's target is to improve the attachment of bags and seats to flight bookings while not hurting overall conversion as additional bags and seats sales are a significant portion of the company's revenue. Your pod has a "mission" that drives decisions and prioritisation, it is a reflection of the team's goals and the strategic role in the site’s (desktop & mobile web) evolution.

### Your team mission

“Enhance the customer travel experience by delivering the most convenient and relevant Seat & Baggage offering.”

## Exercise

Suggest 1 new strategic/large feature and 1 smaller improvement that fits the team’s targets and mission, explaining the following points:

- WHAT you would do: describe the features / improvements
- WHY you would do it: describe your reasoning and how you would evaluate the business benefit
- HOW you would shape it:
  - Define stakeholders that need to be involved
  - Define the metrics to track its success
  - Identify potential risk
  - Consider how you would involve the development team

Provide the relevant materials for taking those features into development (Product Development Requirements document, mockups/wireframes, acceptance criteria, etc.)

Prepare an executive summary that highlights all these components and that can be presented in PowerPoint mode in 15 minutes, including an overview of the process, the key proposals and the rational behind those decisions, the approach that was followed and the priorities that were set, and a set of recommendations and next steps.

Then provide a list of potential questions that can be asked as follow ups.
